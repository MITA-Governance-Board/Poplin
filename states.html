Poplin for States

Poplin is the thread between modules

Poplin's standardized definition of data formats and data transfer methods enables modularity by allowing vendors to focus on individual modules, rather than having to build an entire MMIS system. When your Claims module knows exactly how to communicate with your Pharmacy module, you don't have to worry about being locked into a single vendor.

Get Involved

There are a few ways you can contribute to Poplin:

  • Join our weekly call - contact the administrator for an invite
  • Submit general questions to the Project Poplin email list
  • Join our slack channel and its ongoing conversation threads
  • Help spread the word about Poplin by retweeting major announcements
