Since the Poplin team is spread across multiple organizations, we communicate using a few different channels:

  • If you have general questions about the Poplin APIs, please feel free to email us at info@projectpoplin.org.
  • Every Friday at 1pm Eastern Time, we join a group call to check in with each other. Contact the administrator for an invite.
  • We use slack to coordinate conversations outside of our regularly scheduled calls. If you would like to join our Slack channel, contact the administrator.
  • We send out periodic announcements on the Project Poplin twitter account
