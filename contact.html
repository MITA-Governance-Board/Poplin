Since the Poplin team is spread across multiple organizations, we communicate using a few different channels:

  • If you have general questions about Poplin, please feel free to email us at info@projectpoplin.org.
  • We use slack to coordinate conversations outside of our regularly scheduled calls. If you would like to join our Slack channel, contact the administrator.
  • All code and documentation (including meeting notes in the wiki) are available on our Github site.
