noun: a durable, plain-woven fabric, typically a lightweight cotton with a simple weave

Poplin fabric is often used to create scrubs for clinical staff. The Poplin project seeks to develop a cohesive, free, open-source reference architecture for states to use in developing a modern, modular Medicaid system. It is our hope that the Poplin project serves as the underlying connective fabric for future Medicaid systems.
